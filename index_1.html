Korea's convenience stores are more than snack stops. For millions of students and workers, they are the primary way to eat on a tight schedule and budget. With 55,000 stores nationwide (CU, GS25, 7-Eleven, Emart 24) open 24/7, products like Carbonara Buldak, Banana Milk, and Triangle Kimbap have become daily staples — and global icons.

"Coupang is a rocket delivery service!" Korean convenience stores are nodes in a vast delivery network — CU parcel drop-off, GS25 × Coupang Eats, half-price delivery. At the center is Coupang, Korea's largest e-commerce company, which promises same-day or next-morning Rocket Delivery nationwide. For consumers, it's seamless. For workers behind it, the reality is very different.
